# Lex-Mitra
Lex-Mitra: An AI-Powered Multilingual Legal Shield for Indian Farmers. Built with Node.js and Azure AI (Vision OCR + OpenAI) to translate complex legal documents into simplified, empathetic "Suraksha Reports" in native languages.

**Bridging the Justice Gap with AI-Driven Legal Transparency.**

### üìñ The Problem
In India, legal jargon is often a barrier to justice, especially for farmers and rural citizens. Legal documents are complex, intimidating, and rarely available in local dialects. This leads to a lack of "Samjhaav" (Understanding) and leaves people vulnerable.

### üí° The Solution
Lex-Mitra is a "Multilingual Legal Shield." It allows users to upload photos or PDFs of legal papers. The system then:
1. **Extracts Text:** Uses **Azure AI Document Intelligence** to read handwritten or printed text.
2. **Simplifies:** Uses **Azure OpenAI (GPT-4)** to break down clauses into plain, empathetic language.
3. **Protects:** Generates a **"Suraksha Report"** highlighting potential risks and rights in Hindi, Marathi, or English.
